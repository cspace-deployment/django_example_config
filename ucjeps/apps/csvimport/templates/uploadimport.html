    Caveat utilizator!

    Importing into CollectionSpace is not for the faint of heart. Even though you have a nice, clean "flat file" in the form of a spreadsheet or .csv file you are not out of the woods at all.

    It is highly recommended that you read the documentation if you run into even the slightest difficulty!

    • You are encouraged to experiment first. There is a workflow to allow you to work out the inevitable bugs efficiently: Count first (this will ensure the app undertstands your file format); then Validate (which will check the actuall content of the columns; These two steps are quick and easy even for large files and can save you trouble going forward.
    • then try to Upload.
    • If you are wondering how to match your columns to CSpace records, try clicking Show Configuration below to see which fields this webapp knows about.
    • Fill in this form from top to bottom. Pretty much everything needs to be filled in.
    • Ensure you have all the bits needed before you try: if updating, you'll need to know what column to specify for matching rows to CSpace records;
